Fettuccine Alfredo

Description

A fettuccine Alfredo recipe should never include cream; the silky sauce comes from an emulsion of grated Parmesan, melted butter, and starchy pasta water.

Ingredients

  • 12 ounces fettuccine or other long pasta
  • Kosher salt
  • ¼ cup (½ stick) unsalted butter, cut into small pieces
  • ¾ cup finely grated Parmesan, plus more for serving
  • Freshly ground black pepper

Steps

  1. Cook pasta in a large pot of boiling salted water, stirring occasionally, until very al dente.
  2. Drain, reserving 2 cups pasta cooking liquid.
  3. Transfer 1 cup pasta cooking liquid to a large skillet.
  4. Bring to a gentle simmer, then whisk in butter, no more than 1 tablespoon at a time, until melted.
  5. Whisking constantly, gradually add cheese, making sure it’s completely melted and incorporated before adding more.
  6. Add pasta and toss to coat, adding more pasta cooking liquid as needed, until sauce blankets noodles completely.
  7. Serve topped with pepper and more cheese.
